Who We Are

About Us

The Bookmarked Life is a free and open library dedicated to fostering lifelong learning and curiosity. We curate a diverse collection of books and resources, making knowledge accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

We democratize access to information and stories, empowering individuals to explore, learn, and grow. Literature and knowledge should be freely available, breaking down barriers to education and personal development.

As a community-driven initiative, we rely on passionate volunteers and contributors who share our vision for open access and inclusivity.

Help Us Grow

The Bookmarked Life thrives thanks to volunteers who believe in the transformative power of reading. Join us in one of the following ways:

  • Developers: Enhance our platform, add new features, and improve user experience.
  • Content Curators: Discover and add new books, ensuring our library remains diverse and relevant.
  • Community Managers: Grow our presence on social media, engage with readers, and build a vibrant community.
  • Translators: Help us reach a global audience by translating content into different languages.

You can also support us by sharing The Bookmarked Life with your network, inviting others to explore and contribute.
